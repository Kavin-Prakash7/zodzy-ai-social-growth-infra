Data Ingestion & Signal Collection (Foundation Layer)

The system begins by continuously collecting raw platform data.

YouTube Info Collector
Gathers complete video metadata including views, watch time, engagement, publish time, and channel context.

Like Scraper
Calculates like-to-view ratios to measure true audience interest beyond surface-level views.

Duration Scraper
Analyzes watch behavior to identify optimal video lengths that maximize retention and reach.

Favorite Scraper
Detects high-value content by tracking save and revisit behavior, indicating long-term interest.

Title Scraper
Identifies high-performing title structures that drive clicks and trust.

Published-At Agent
Determines optimal upload timing based on audience activity and algorithm response windows.

Tags Scraper
Extracts and ranks top-performing tags to improve discoverability and algorithm alignment.

Comments Scraper
Analyzes sentiment, emotion, and intent within comments to understand audience reaction.

Agentic Dashboard Orchestration

What This System in Dashboard Does ?

This system breaks down content performance into clear, real-time signals that explain why something is working or not, instead of just showing views or likes.

Each signal answers one specific question about audience behavior or algorithm response.

1. Duration 

SWEETSPOT
Shows whether the content length is optimal for audience attention.

RETENTION
Measures how well viewers stay engaged throughout the content.

LIFT
Tracks improvement or drop in engagement compared to past content.

COMPLETION
Shows how many viewers finish the content fully.



2. Views 

VELOCITY
How fast views are increasing right now.

PULSE
The momentum of growth‚Äîwhether views are accelerating or slowing.

DRIFT
Detects whether interest is fading over time.

REACH
How many new people the content is reaching.



3.  Publish Timing 

WINDOW
Identifies if the content was published at the right time.

HALFLIFE
How long the content stays relevant after publishing.

BOOST
Detects short-term algorithm promotion.

RHYTHM
Shows consistency and timing alignment with audience behavior.


4. Likes 

APPEAL
How attractive the content is at first glance.

LOYALTY
Whether the same audience keeps liking consistently.

AMPLIFY
How likely people are to support the content publicly.

SIGNAL
Overall strength of positive audience response.


5. Comments 

SPARK
How quickly conversations start.

ECHO
Whether discussions continue or die quickly.

PULL
How strongly the content invites opinions or responses.

HEAT
Intensity of audience reaction.



6. Title 

GRAB
Does the title catch attention immediately?

ALIGN
Does the title match the content expectations?

PULL
Does the title make people curious enough to click?

TRUST
Does the title feel honest and credible?



7. Thumbnail

HOOK
Does the thumbnail stop people from scrolling?

STORY
Does it visually explain the content?

CONTRAST
Does it stand out among other content?

SIGNAL
Overall effectiveness of the thumbnail.
